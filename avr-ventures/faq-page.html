The Basics

What is Day Trading?

In it's simplest form day trading is looking at a certain market whether it be the regular stock market or foreign exchange market and quickly making decision whether the price will go up or go down. Most day traders will spend anywhere from 2-5 hours in front of their computers taking trades. Those trades will usually last for only a couple minutes to a full day at most. Day trading is another way you can make a secondary source of income however it can be very risky. Trade at your own risk

Can you make a full time income with Day Trading?

You certainly can! Day traders can make anwhere from zero dollars to thousands every single day. What you have to remember is risk managment. This is a common practiced used by traders to make sure that they maintain a certain ammount of money in their account so they can continue making money. Another way to look at it is percentages. Some day trades are making 0% to all the way up to 1000% a year. If you were to have $20,000 and you make 200% in a single year. You Would have made $40,000 that year. For some that is a full time income. The more money you have the easier it is to make money. However you do not need a ton to make a full time income and can quickly build up your capital over the years.
