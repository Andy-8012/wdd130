  • What is Technical Analysis?

    Technical analysis is using candle stick patterns to determine if the price will continue upward or move downward. For many people this is the prefered method because it is very easy to do this for free.

    Advantages of Technical Analysis

    The main advantage that comes from technical analysis is everyone has access to it. All you need is a charting software that tracks the price of the market. There are many free resources on line.

    Disadvantages of Technical Analysis

    The main disadvantage to technical analysis is how many patterns there are. It take a long time to master each pattern that shows up sometimes thousands of hours.

  • What are Indicators?

    There are two main types of indicators. First, lagging indicators which are usually free. Seccond, live indicators which are usually a paid service.

    Both types of indicators use an algorythm to predict which way the market is going to go. These aren't used as much because a lot of people beleive that since they are more commonly used they can be manipulated especailly lagging indicators

    Advantages of Indicators

    The main advantage from using indicators is they are easy for a beginer to read. All you are doing are looking at lines pointed up or down that tell you which way the market will move.

    Disadvantages of Indicators

    The main disadvantage and concern for using indicators are there are so many out there how do you know which one to choose. Also with their easy readibility they can also be easily manipulated. More expereinced traders could use these idicators to determine what beginners are thinking and do the opposite to make even more money.
